🛡️ WPlace AutoBOT - Defend Mode

The defend functionality has been successfully implemented! Here's how to use the new defend mode:

🎯 How Defend Mode Works
1. Scan Colors First
Click "Scan Colors" to enable defend mode buttons
2. Select Defend Area
Click "Select Defend Area" → Place a pixel on the canvas to mark the top-left corner of your 50x50 defend zone
3. Start Defending
Click "Start Defending" to begin monitoring and protecting your area
4. Automatic Protection
Bot scans every 2 seconds and repairs any "attacked" pixels using available charges
